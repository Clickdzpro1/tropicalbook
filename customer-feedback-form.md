Thank you for choosing Tropical Parking! Your feedback helps us improve. This survey takes only 30 seconds to complete.

**Leave a review and get 10% off your next booking!**

## After Creating Form

### 1. Get the Form Link
- Click "Send" button
- Copy the link
- Should look like: `https://forms.gle/xxxxxxxxxx`

### 2. Shorten the Link
- Go to bit.ly
- Create shortened URL: `bit.ly/tropicalfeedback`
- Use this in SMS messages and emails

### 3. Set Up Response Spreadsheet
- Click "Responses" tab in Google Forms
- Click green Excel icon to create linked spreadsheet
- This auto-populates with all responses
- Share with Marc and Mustapha

### 4. Create Response Dashboard (Optional but Recommended)
In the Google Sheet, create a simple dashboard:

**Summary Tab:**
```
Total Responses: =COUNTA(Sheet1!A:A)-1
Average Rating: =AVERAGE(Sheet1!B:B)
5-Star Reviews: =COUNTIF(Sheet1!B:B,"⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐")
4-Star Reviews: =COUNTIF(Sheet1!B:B,"⭐⭐⭐⭐")
Would Return: =COUNTIF(Sheet1!E:E,"Definitely yes")
Avg Shuttle Wait: [Calculate average from column C]
```

## How to Use the Feedback

### Weekly Review (Every Monday):
1. **Check response count** - Goal: 20+ responses/week
2. **Review average rating** - Goal: 4.5+ stars
3. **Read negative feedback** - Respond to any 3-star or below
4. **Identify patterns** - Are multiple people mentioning same issue?
5. **Celebrate wins** - Share 5-star reviews with team

### Monthly Analysis:
1. **Shuttle wait time trends** - Is it improving?
2. **Return likelihood** - What % say "Definitely yes"?
3. **Common complaints** - Top 3 issues to address
4. **Best reviews** - Add to website testimonials

### Immediate Actions:
- **1-2 star reviews** → Call customer within 24 hours
- **3 star reviews** → Send apology email + discount
- **4-5 star reviews** → Ask for Google review
- **"Would not return"** → Reach out to understand why
